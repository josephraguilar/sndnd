## AI Summary
The chaos from [[campaign/npcs/Tibit\|Tibit]]’s graveyard blunder ended when **[[campaign/npcs/Grusksa\|Grusksa]]**, the half-orc gravekeeper, emerged and used a **magical amulet** to pacify the undead guardian, commanding it back beneath the earth. Peace returned to the graveyard by dawn, and the party continued their journey south with **[[campaign/npcs/Tibit\|Tibit]]** now traveling alongside them. Along the road, they learned of a fortified trading hub called **[[campaign/locations/6_sites/Grud-Thacks Cradle\|Grud-Thacks Cradle]]**, known for taxing all travelers who entered.

While en route, the party encountered a **slave caravan** escorted by orcs under the banner of the **[[campaign/factions/Veruz Rexna\|Veruz Rexna]]**, accompanied by a **Cedar Witch** and **Paladins of [[campaign/npcs/Iuz\|Iuz]]**. After a fierce battle, the group slew the witch and her guards, freed the slaves, and captured one surviving paladin named **[[campaign/npcs/Svayne\|Svayne]]**. Interrogation revealed that the caravan had come from **[[campaign/locations/4_cities_islands/Caer Callidier\|Caer Callidier]]** and was headed to the **mines**, delivering dwarven and gnomish slaves marked by **[[campaign/factions/The Three\|The Three]]**. The witch, **[[campaign/npcs/Kragatha\|Kragatha]]**, had been meeting with **[[campaign/npcs/Maghla Blackfang\|Maghla Blackfang]]** under orders from **[[campaign/npcs/Rotten Agnis Worm Wiggle\|Rotten Agnis Worm Wiggle]]**, one of [[campaign/factions/The Three\|The Three]], to discuss the defense of **[[campaign/locations/6_sites/Galdur Towers\|Galdur Towers]]** — a stronghold located in **[[campaign/npcs/Calimshan\|Calimshan]]**.

To extract further answers, the party used **Speak with Dead** on [[campaign/npcs/Kragatha\|Kragatha]]’s corpse. After learning the truth, **[[campaign/players/Obold\|Obold]]** took matters into his own hands — forcing [[campaign/npcs/Svayne\|Svayne]] to **pledge his soul to [[campaign/npcs/Baphomet\|Baphomet]]** before executing him without mercy, a chilling act that left the rest of the group uneasy as they pressed on toward [[campaign/locations/6_sites/Grud-Thacks Cradle\|Grud-Thacks Cradle]].

### **The Slave Caravan**

on the way to Grud-Thacks Cradle, we came across a **caravan of orcs** bearing the banners of the Veruz Rexna, accompanied by a Cedar Witch and a group of **chained slaves**.

After a tense and bloody battle, we freed the captives and captured one of the surviving Paladins of Iuz for questioning. During the interrogation, we learned the following:

- The caravan had come from Caer Callidier to meet with someone connected to the **Order of Iuz**.
    
- The slaves were being **transported to the mines**.
    
- They were **marked by The Three
    
- Their Witch’s name was Kragatha.
- They had met with The Three prior to leaving
    
- The slaves were mostly **dwarves of different clans and origins**, along with several **gnomes**.

The paladins name was Svayne, and the one who was slain was named Magnus

The party used speak with dead to ask Kragatha questions
- who were you meeting?
	- Maghla Blackfang
- Why were you meeting with them?
	- They were sent to talk about protecting the Galdur Towers
- Which member of the three sent them.
	- Rotten Agnis Worm Wiggle
- Where is Galdur tower?
	- in Calimshan

While the party is trying to decide what to do with the remaining paladin Svayne, Obold forces him to pledge his soul to Baphomet, then executes him in cold blood.
