## AI Summary
The party escaped the sealed chamber thanks to [[campaign/players/Gunther\|Gunther]]’s brute strength and found themselves in a prison area filled with cells. Inside, they freed several captives — three halflings, a human named [[campaign/npcs/Thwayne\|Thwayne]], a dwarven woman named [[campaign/npcs/Holda\|Holda]], and a powerful tiefling named [[campaign/npcs/Salyx\|Salyx]], who turned out to be [[campaign/npcs/Thexus\|Thexus]]’s brother and easily overpowered both [[campaign/players/Obold\|Obold]] and [[campaign/players/Gunther\|Gunther]]. After some playful banter between [[campaign/players/Alias\|Alias]] and [[campaign/npcs/Salyx\|Salyx]], the group continued deeper into the temple in search of the missing books. They eventually encountered two of [[campaign/factions/The Three\|The Three]] hags along with members of the [[campaign/factions/Shag Dominica\|Shag Dominica]], leading to a fierce battle in which the party emerged victorious, killing the cultists and one of the sea hags and finding the two books they were in search of.

## Log
The party escaped the trapped room through the brute force of Gunther. 
Trying to escape the party made it to a room with lots of cells. We unlocked one and found 3 slave halflings and one tiefling named Salyx who was able to wrestle both Obold and Gunther. Salyx is Thexus's brother. Amongst the other slaves we found was a human named Thwayne and a dwarven woman named Holda. 

Alias playfully argued with Salyx for a bit before the group left to find the books. Eventually the party entered a room with two of The Three hags and some members of the Shag Dominica. 

A fight breaks out in which the party defeats the members of the Shag Dominica as well as one of the sea hags.
