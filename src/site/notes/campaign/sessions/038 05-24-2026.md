### Into the Tower

One harpy had slipped through the tower door before the Scorned Eight could cut her down. They followed through the entrance of [[campaign/npcs/Rotten Agnis Worm Wiggle\|Agnus the Maw]]'s Galder Tower — and did not arrive anywhere recognizable.

The tower's interior was a pocket dimension. The party found themselves standing in a vast forest, enormous and still, with fog between the trees. The wrongness of the place was immediate and physical: the ground underfoot gave slightly with each step, soft and faintly warm, like wet flesh.

---

### Tarian

Somewhere in the wood, a tree moved with intention and then spoke.

[[campaign/npcs/Tarian\|Tarian]] was enormous — not quite a full ent, but something between. He told them he had once been a wood elf, before the hag's power had taken him and bound him into this form. He was not hostile. He warned the party that they were in danger inside this place — that the forest was not passive, and that something was already aware of them.

Before he could say more, the attack came.

---

### The Tree Spirits

Tree spirits emerged from the wood and went straight for [[campaign/players/Obold\|Obold]]. They were strange fighters — when they were cut, the wounds did not bleed blood. They bled sap, and from each wound small flowers bloomed, so that the fallen spirits left trails of growth across the wet-flesh ground.

Other full ents were present in the forest as well, though their role in the fight is not fully recorded.

---

### The Descent

At some point during the party's time in the pocket dimension, a staircase appeared or descended — the circumstances are unclear from available notes. It led to a deeper level within the pocket dimension. The party worked through whatever lay on that level and eventually found their way to [[campaign/npcs/Rotten Agnis Worm Wiggle\|Agnus the Maw]]'s lair.

The session ended there.
