## AI Summary
### Voyage from Llorgh

Immediately after departing Llorgh, we were approached by a vessel belonging to Gwyneth. We sailed past one another peacefully. Later that same night, we passed another of the King’s ships.

During the journey, we endured a powerful storm. Thorin used **Calm Waters** to steady the sea while Aelius skillfully navigated us through the worst of it.

By midday of the third day, we entered a dense fog bank. Before nightfall, something massive struck the underside of our ship. The creature surged from the depths and latched onto the hull — revealing itself as an **Aboleth**.

Kennecko dealt devastating damage (98 damage in total), shooting it multiple times. The creature eventually released its grip and retreated into the depths.

When it detached, Kennecko, Lois, and Ofie were thrown overboard. In a dramatic first, Ofie transformed into a **giant eagle** and rescued them, carrying everyone safely back to the ship.

---

### Arrival at Caer Callidyr

We arrived at the port of Caer Callidyr and immediately noticed changes:

- No slaves among the trade
    
- No auction blocks present
    

Aelius convinced the dockmaster, **Nate Jones**, to reduce our docking fees (half off) by mentioning our friendship with Callista. Aelius also tipped Nate to keep a close eye on our ship so the crew could rest safely on shore.

---

### Crew Celebration

The party and crew went to a tavern (The Flaggen and Crow) to celebrate. Obold organized a bucking contest using Ramses. Everyone took turns trying to ride him, and La’Poopoo lasted the longest.

After celebrating with ale and festivities, the party returned to the ship to rest.

---

### Summoned by Callista

The following morning, three adventurers approached our ship, informing us that Callista had summoned us to the **Wanton Wench**.

We were escorted and seated in a reserved section. Laila, the barkeep, brought us drinks and breakfast while we waited.

Fifteen minutes later, Callista arrived.

She appeared far more affluent than before, dressed in finer clothing and no longer portraying herself as a poor innkeeper. Armed escorts accompanied her.

She warmly greeted us as great friends, congratulating us on our accomplishments in Valeen and the destruction of the Uganothi clan.

She revealed she now controls multiple properties on Alaron and offered us four possible bases of operations:
