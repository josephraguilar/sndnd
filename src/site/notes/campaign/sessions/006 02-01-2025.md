## AI Summary
After meeting Queen [[campaign/npcs/Melian\|Melian]] of [[campaign/npcs/Niavara\|Niavara]] and learning she had earned her impressive armor by slaying a dragon, the party spent the night in [[campaign/locations/4_cities_islands/Waterdeep\|Waterdeep]] before preparing to continue their journey. The next day, they went shopping and reaffirmed that [[campaign/players/Alias\|Alias]] would serve as captain of _The [[Bitch’s Scorn\|Bitch’s Scorn]]_—a role even [[campaign/players/Obold\|Obold]] agreed was well deserved. The crew set sail toward [[campaign/locations/2_regions & archipelagos/Velen\|Velen]], stopping briefly in [[campaign/locations/4_cities_islands/Baldurs Gate\|Baldurs Gate]] for supplies. During the stop, [[campaign/players/Obold\|Obold]] set out an alms box to gather followers for [[campaign/npcs/Baphomet\|Baphomet]], and [[campaign/players/Lois\|Louis]] followed some converts to complete their initiation into worship. In gratitude, she received a blessed amulet, to [[campaign/players/Obold\|Obold]]’s quiet envy. With supplies restocked and their faiths renewed, the party once again took to the seas, determined to reach [[campaign/locations/2_regions & archipelagos/Velen\|Velen]] without further delay.

## Log
The party speaks with Melian and ask where she got her cool armor from. She said that she got it from slaying a dragon. She leaves and the party rests for a night.

The next day we go shopping and then set sail. The party (especially Obold) determine that Alias is considered the captain of the ship. (obvious choice, honestly). 

We set sail for Velen and stop at Baldurs Gate on the way for supplies. Obold sets out his alms box and gets a couple people to pay alms to Baphomet. Lois follows some of them and takes them to a place of worship to fully convert them. After doing so she receives an amulet in a show of appreciation for her service (Obold is jealous).

The party then sets sail one last time for Velen and nothing is going to stop them now!
