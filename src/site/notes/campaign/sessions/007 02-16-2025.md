## AI Summary
As the party departed [[campaign/locations/4_cities_islands/Baldurs Gate\|Baldurs Gate]], they were intercepted by the _[[campaign/items/Black Fang\|Black Fang]]_, captained by [[campaign/npcs/Xanaphia\|Xanaphia]]. She warned them that the [[campaign/factions/Dark Daggers\|Dark Daggers]] had discovered their movements and dispatched assassins to eliminate them, convincing the crew to return to [[campaign/locations/4_cities_islands/Baldurs Gate\|Baldurs Gate]] to negotiate directly. Once there, they were escorted by Dark Dagger agents to the _[[campaign/locations/6_sites/Velvet Veil\|Velvet Veil]]_—a lavish brothel that secretly served as the guild’s base. Inside, they met [[campaign/npcs/Laysora\|Laysora]], the proprietor, and [[campaign/npcs/Valan\|Valana]], the leader of the [[campaign/factions/Dark Daggers\|Dark Daggers]], who revealed that the party had effectively stolen _The [[Bitch’s Scorn\|Bitch’s Scorn]]_. However, Valana offered them a deal: keep the ship if they dealt with [[campaign/locations/4_cities_islands/Mintarin\|campaign/locations/4_cities_islands/Mintarin]], a wizard in the [[campaign/locations/2_regions & archipelagos/Cloud Peak Mountains\|Cloud Peak Mountains]] who had cursed the region to harm any Dark Dagger. Accepting the mission, the party left for the mountains, battling owlbears and great apes along the way.

## Log
As the party is leaving Baldurs Gate, we are approached by another ship and it is captained by none other than Xanaphia! The ship is named the Black Fang, and Xanaphia is the captain of it. Xanaphia informs us that the Dark Daggers saw our ship leaving Baldurs Gate and sent assassins out to intercept us. She convinced us to head back to Baldurs Gate to speak with the Dark Daggers before heading to Velen. 

Once we dock in Baldurs gate again we are approached by some members of the Dark Daggers who take us to the Velvet Veil, their secret hideout. The entrance is an opulent yet unassuming brothel. A half orc female, a human male, a human female, and a black male tiefling led us to Laysora, who owns the Velvet Veil. We learn that Valan runs the Dark Daggers.

Valan informs us that we technically stole the Bitch's Scorn, and want it back. We are offered another option to keep the ship though. The Dark Daggers are having anissue with magic. In the Cloud Peak Mountains to the east of Baldurs Gate there is a powerful wizard named Zorath the Veilbinder. The dark daggers had stolen from him previously, and now the dark daggers cannot approach the area, as a protective curse/barrier inflicts pain on anyone bearing the mark of the dark daggers.

Valan offers us our ship back if we kill the Zorath or somehow remove the barrier/curse. The party agrees and sets off to the Cloud Peak Mountains. On our way we kill some owlbears and some large apes.
