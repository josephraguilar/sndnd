## Log
After the party found the Book of Iuz, the Book of Necromancy, and the Book on 8th Level of Hell they tried to escape, but as we exited the underbelly of the Temple of the high Cleric, Arch Bishop Corin Mag was waiting on us!

We got in yet another fight and were victorious, felling Corin Mag. 

The party learns that Salyx, Thexus, and Zae are all related and Zae and Salyx are lovers. (I assume someone is an in-law)

Thexus and Zae walked in after we defeated Arch Bishop Corin Mag and we gave them the Galdrabrock

Kira Swan walked in after we defeated the Arch Bishop, and we gave her the Book of Necromancy and the Book on the 8th level of Hell as promised. 

As the party is trying to leave the temple there is a huge battle going on outside between the Velvet Blade, the Gloomshadow, Slack's goblins, and the paladins of Iuz. 

WE MUST ESCAPE!

Ophie goes invisible to hijack us a wagon. The party uses it to crash through the battle. We were chased by another wagon fill of Veruz Rexna, but we let out oil slick and magic thorns and the party made quick work of them. As they were going down, a witch that was with them threw a forebolt at us, catching the wagon on fire and knocking out Lois and Kaneko. The party was able to get out of the wagon and defend them long enough to become stable and we took off to the woods to escape. Unfortunately we were met with a cliffside, our getaway skiff sitting below.  We all decided to risk it and jump, thankfully we made it safely. We rowed back to the docks where Irlan Liaric was waiting for us on the Bitch's Scorn with someone at his side. We soon realized that it was none other than Kalista!
