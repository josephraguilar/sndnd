# [[campaign/locations/6_sites/Tidewatch Bastion\|Tidewatch Bastion]]

The [[campaign/factions/Scorned Eight\|Scorned Eight]]'s home base. A cliffside tower keep on the north side of [[campaign/locations/4_cities_islands/Caer Callidier\|Caer Callidier]]'s island, overlooking a small sheltered bay — remote, secure, and with private docking access for [[campaign/items/Bitch's Scorn\|Bitch's Scorn]]. Chosen by the party in session [[campaign/sessions/034 03-01-2026\|034 03-01-2026]] from several options offered by [[campaign/npcs/Kalista\|Kalista]].

*Approximately half a day's travel from [[campaign/locations/4_cities_islands/Caer Callidier\|Caer Callidier]] proper.*

---

## The Grounds

The Bastion has been significantly improved since the party took possession:

- **Tower keep** — the main structure, once crumbling, now repaired. Includes a basement laboratory claimed by [[campaign/players/Kormak\|Kormak]].
- **Training yard** — built by [[campaign/players/Thorin\|Thorin]]: timber posts, rope targets, sandbag dummies, rotating shield targets on wagon axles, and a stone-outlined sparring circle.
- **Barn** — houses a small forge in the far corner, built by Thorin from salvaged smithy equipment. A brewing station occupies another corner with fermentation barrels and copper kettles.
- **Fields** — barley and wheat planted by [[campaign/npcs/Tobin Bramblefoot\|Tobin Bramblefoot]] under enthusiastic instruction from the dwarves. Intended for the eventual dwarven brewery.
- **Small bay below** — managed by [[campaign/npcs/Derek Coilrope\|Derek Coilrope]], who oversees the landing dock and ferries supplies and people between the Bastion and Caer Callidier.
