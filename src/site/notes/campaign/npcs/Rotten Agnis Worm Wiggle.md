- **[[campaign/sessions/037 05-02-2026\|037 05-02-2026]]** — Her Galder Tower was spotted glowing with cold green light through the bog fog. The party fought through the tower's sentinels and stood at its entrance as the session ended.
- **[[campaign/sessions/038 05-24-2026\|038 05-24-2026]]** — The party entered her Galder Tower and were transported into a pocket dimension — a vast, still forest where the ground felt like wet flesh. - **[[campaign/sessions/039 06-14-2026\|039 06-14-2026]]** — Confronted in the depths of her pocket dimension lair, clinging to the walls in a horrific spider-wood form and pulsing waves of blight across the party. [[campaign/players/Thorin\|Thorin]] kept her giant ent companion banished throughout; [[campaign/players/Obold\|Obold]] rode her giant raven before [[campaign/players/Kaneko\|Kaneko]] shot it down. [[campaign/players/Ofie\|Ofie]] and [[campaign/players/Obold\|Obold]] struck the killing blow together, and her death caused the pocket dimension to collapse.
