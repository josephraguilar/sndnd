# [[campaign/npcs/Derek Coilrope\|Derek Coilrope]]
Spent most of his life ferrying cargo between the Moonshade Isles and the mainland. Years of working rough seas have given him a steady temperament — nothing seems to rattle him. Oversees the small landing dock and handles ferrying supplies and people between the Bastion and [[campaign/locations/4_cities_islands/Caer Callidier\|Caer Callidier]].
