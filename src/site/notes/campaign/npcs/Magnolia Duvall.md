# [[campaign/npcs/Magnolia Duvall\|Magnolia Duvall]]

## Personality
**Traits:** Charismatic, flirty, and unhurried — she moves through the world on her own terms and makes no apologies for it. A southern drawl and easy confidence that makes people trust her before they've thought about whether they should.
**Ideals:** Freedom and luck — a follower of [[campaign/npcs/Tymora\|Tymora]], she believes fortune favors those bold enough to reach for it.
**Bonds:** Her brother [[campaign/npcs/Bo Duvall\|Bo Duvall]] and her ship the [[campaign/items/Morning Afterglow\|Morning Afterglow]] are her constants. The party — especially [[campaign/players/Alias\|Alias]] — occupy a warm but unresolved space somewhere between contacts and friends.
**Flaws:** She always has somewhere else to be.
