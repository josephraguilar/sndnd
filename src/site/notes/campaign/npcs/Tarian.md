# [[campaign/npcs/Tarian\|Tarian]]

A wood elf transformed by [[campaign/npcs/Rotten Agnis Worm Wiggle\|Agnus the Maw]] into a half-ent form, trapped within the pocket dimension inside her Galder Tower on [[campaign/locations/3_nations/Oman\|Oman]]. He awoke and spoke to the Scorned Eight upon their arrival, warning them they were in danger before tree spirits attacked the party.
