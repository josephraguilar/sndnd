# [[campaign/npcs/Elthyr Velaryon\|Elthyr Velaryon]]
A high-born Sylvan Elf, his skin carrying the faint blue hue of the Sylvan bloodline. He and Zydaria, then a drow high priestess of Lolth, conducted a secret relationship forbidden by the Spider Queen. When Lolth discovered the truth, she commanded Zydaria to end it. She refused. Lolth sent assassins; Elthyr was killed on the surface. Zydaria was subsequently bound into a weapon as punishment.
