- **[[campaign/sessions/033 02-13-2026\|033 02-13-2026]]** — Grudrack challenged [[campaign/players/Obold\|Obold]] to a ritual one-on-one duel inside a circle of glowing glyphs while an [[campaign/factions/Uganathi Clan\|Uganathi Clan]] warrior held [[campaign/npcs/Kaela\|Kaela]] at knifepoint to prevent the party from interfering.
