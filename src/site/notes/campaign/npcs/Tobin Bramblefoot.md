# [[campaign/npcs/Tobin Bramblefoot\|Tobin Bramblefoot]]
Grew up tending orchards and gardens throughout the Moonshades. Now maintains the fields behind the Bastion, including the barley and wheat planted under instruction from the dwarves for their planned brewery. Proud of every successful harvest.

## Personality
**Traits:** Cheerful and endlessly optimistic. Loves animals and plants equally. Talks constantly to the plants he tends, giving them encouragement as he works.
