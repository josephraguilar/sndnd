## Log Mentions

- **[[campaign/sessions/010 03-30-2025\|010 03-30-2025]]** — The party found Grolgath hiding beneath a bridge in the Ogre Wastelands, an exile from the [[campaign/factions/Bloodfang Tribe\|Bloodfang Tribe]] who had objected to his clan's atrocities and was left for dead. After the party healed him, he guided them west through dangerous terrain and pointed out ritual flowers used in [[campaign/npcs/Gruumsh\|Gruumsh]] worship nearby.
- **[[campaign/sessions/014 06-14-2025\|014 06-14-2025]]** — Grolgath warned the party about an oncoming Ash Storm and led them to shelter in a derelict farmhouse, protecting the group from the deadly tempest sweeping through the wastelands.
- **[[campaign/sessions/017 08-10-2025\|017 08-10-2025]]** — When the party was ambushed by Bloodfang Tribe orcs, the attacking leader recognized Grolgath from his former clan, stirring deep shame. After the battle, Grolgath confided his guilt over his exile to the narrator, who reassured him he had a permanent place with the group.
- **[[campaign/sessions/019 09-07-2025\|019 09-07-2025]]** — Following the rescue of [[Vista\|Vista]] and the liberation of [[campaign/locations/4_cities_islands/Gorvathcha\|Gorvakutha]], Grolgath departed from the party with grim resolve, announcing his intent to return and challenge his former Bloodfang Tribe leader for the chieftainship.
