# [[campaign/npcs/Merwin Greenbranch\|Merwin Greenbranch]]
Travels the Moonshade Isles with her band, performing in towns and villages and carrying Firbolg songs wherever people will listen. When she recognized [[campaign/players/Ofie\|Ofie]] in the market, she ended her song mid-verse. The conversation was brief but full of laughter — she and her band left nearly giddy, promising to spread tales of Princess Ofie's travels with the Scorned Eight.

## Personality
**Traits:** Reverent toward Firbolg royalty. Excited and nearly giddy at meeting Ofie. Dedicated to preserving and spreading Firbolg music and song.
