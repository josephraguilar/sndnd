- **[[campaign/sessions/029 12-21-2025\|029 12-21-2025]]** — [[campaign/npcs/Thavgrak\|Thavrak]], Obold's uncle, claimed while cornered that Kaela was still alive and imprisoned with [[campaign/npcs/Grudrack\|Grudrack]] deep inside the volcano before Obold killed him.
- **[[campaign/sessions/032 02-01-2026\|032 02-01-2026]]** — A frightened prisoner in the dungeon revealed that Kaela had left with [[campaign/npcs/Grudrack\|Grudrack]] only an hour before the party arrived. [[campaign/players/Obold\|Obold]] and [[campaign/players/Ofie\|Ofie]] tracked her scent deeper into the caverns until they emerged on an obsidian platform facing Grudrack directly.
- **[[campaign/sessions/033 02-13-2026\|033 02-13-2026]]** — During [[campaign/players/Obold\|Obold]]'s ritual duel with [[campaign/npcs/Grudrack\|Grudrack]], an [[campaign/factions/Uganathi Clan\|Uganathi Clan]] warrior held Kaela at knifepoint to prevent the party from intervening.
