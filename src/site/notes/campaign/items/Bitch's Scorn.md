# [[campaign/items/Bitch's Scorn\|Bitch's Scorn]]
The ship the party owns (coolest ship in the world, totally wasn't a slave ship)

## Log
Was "stolen" from the [[campaign/factions/Dark Daggers\|Dark Daggers]]. Eventually paid for

## Log Mentions

- **[[campaign/sessions/003 11-24-2024\|003 11-24-2024]]** — After defeating [[campaign/npcs/Arch Bishop Corin Mag\|Arch Bishop Corin Mag]] and escaping through a chaotic battlefield, the party reached the docks and sailed back to the Bitch's Scorn, where [[campaign/npcs/Irlan Liaric\|Irlan Liaric]] and the unexpected return of [[campaign/npcs/Kalista\|Kalista]] awaited them.
- **[[campaign/sessions/004 01-05-2025\|004 01-05-2025]]** — The party departed aboard the Bitch's Scorn and were attacked by a massive [[campaign/monsters/Aboleth\|Aboleth]] that tore a hole in the hull, forcing an emergency landing on [[campaign/npcs/Mintarin\|campaign/npcs/Mintarin]] for repairs.
- **[[campaign/sessions/006 02-01-2025\|006 02-01-2025]]** — After meeting Queen [[campaign/npcs/Melian\|Melian]] of [[campaign/npcs/Niavara\|Niavara]] in [[campaign/locations/4_cities_islands/Waterdeep\|Waterdeep]], the party reaffirmed that [[campaign/players/Alias\|Alias]] would serve as captain of the Bitch's Scorn — a role even [[campaign/players/Obold\|Obold]] agreed was well deserved — before setting sail for [[campaign/locations/2_regions & archipelagos/Velen\|Velen]].
- **[[campaign/sessions/007 02-16-2025\|007 02-16-2025]]** — [[campaign/npcs/Valan\|Valana]], leader of the [[campaign/factions/Dark Daggers\|Dark Daggers]], revealed that the party had effectively stolen the Bitch's Scorn; she offered them permanent ownership if they would deal with [[campaign/locations/4_cities_islands/Mintarin\|campaign/locations/4_cities_islands/Mintarin]], who had cursed the [[campaign/locations/2_regions & archipelagos/Cloud Peak Mountains\|Cloud Peak Mountains]] region against all Dark Daggers.
- **[[campaign/sessions/009 03-16-2025\|009 03-16-2025]]** — After returning to the [[campaign/locations/6_sites/Velvet Veil\|Velvet Veil]] under Dark Dagger escort, the party reclaimed the Bitch's Scorn and learned that the guild had originally enslaved them aboard her — [[campaign/players/Obold\|Obold]] barely contained his rage until they were safely at sea.
- **[[campaign/sessions/035 03-15-2026\|035 03-15-2026]]** — During the downtime week, [[campaign/players/Alias\|Alias]] repaired and resupplied the Bitch's Scorn at the docks of [[campaign/locations/4_cities_islands/Caer Callidier\|Caer Callidier]] before sailing her around the coast to anchor in the private cove below [[campaign/locations/6_sites/Tidewatch Bastion\|Tidewatch Bastion]].
- **[[campaign/sessions/039 06-14-2026\|039 06-14-2026]]** — As [[campaign/locations/3_nations/Oman\|Oman]] sank, the Bitch's Scorn's crew were driven far out to sea by the surging floodwaters and could not respond; the party was rescued by [[campaign/npcs/Magnolia Duvall\|Magnolia Duvall]] first, and the crew sailed back shortly after once the surge subsided.
