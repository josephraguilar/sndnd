# [[campaign/items/Dimensional Net\|Dimensional Net]]
A large ranged net woven from the hair of celestials and fiends. Looted from the [[campaign/factions/Uganathi Clan\|Uganathi Clan]] stronghold in session [[campaign/sessions/032 02-01-2026\|032 02-01-2026]].

**Properties:**
- +1 to attack rolls
- Aberrations, celestials, and fiends restrained by it cannot escape using alternate movement (teleportation, planar travel, etc.)
