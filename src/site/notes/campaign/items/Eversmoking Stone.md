# [[campaign/items/Eversmoking Stone\|Eversmoking Stone]]
Functionally identical to an Eversmoking Bottle, except the smoke originates from a stone placed inside a bottle. Purchased by [[campaign/players/Obold\|Obold]] from Brannic Copperwave at the Gears of the Tidelord in [[campaign/locations/4_cities_islands/Caer Callidier\|Caer Callidier]] for 500 gold in session [[campaign/sessions/034 03-01-2026\|034 03-01-2026]].
