# [[campaign/items/Z'Daria Nix\|Z'Daria Nix]]
*Also known as Zydaria.*

[[campaign/players/Obold\|Obold]]'s magical halberd, received from [[campaign/npcs/Baphomet\|Baphomet]] in exchange for the [[campaign/items/Book of Iuz\|Book of Iuz]] in session [[campaign/sessions/005 01-19-2025\|005 01-19-2025]].

The weapon is sentient — her soul, that of a former **drow high priestess of Lolth**, was bound into the halberd by Lolth as punishment for refusing to end a forbidden relationship. She would have preferred a saber.

*Note: a separate entry also exists at `campaign/npcs/Z'Daria Nix.md` — this may represent a spirit or entity bound to the weapon.*
