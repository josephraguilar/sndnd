# [[campaign/factions/Scorned Eight\|Scorned Eight]]
Eight adventurers who came together through shared suffering at the hands of slavers. They have since dismantled much of the slave trade's local operation and established Tidewatch Bastion as their base. Currently pursuing three hag sisters believed to be central to ongoing evil in the region.
