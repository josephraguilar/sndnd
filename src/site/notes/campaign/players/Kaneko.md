# [[campaign/players/Kaneko\|Kaneko]]
Kaneko is the party's quiet edge — a wood elf Gloom Stalker ranger who operates best when no one knows he is there. He routinely serves as one of the advance scouts, moving ahead with Vutha to read a room before the party commits, and when he springs an ambush he does it decisively: three arrows, first raptor, instant kill. His combat style is defined by positioning and burst damage — he harries rear lines, snipes isolated targets to relieve teammates, and dealt a single-encounter total of 98 damage to an Aboleth that was threatening the ship, driving it off alone. He is not particularly social; while the party delivered the cursed crate to the Dark Daggers, Kaneko slipped away independently to gather information at the Dockside Bear, preferring his own channels to the group's. He does not seek the spotlight and is rarely at the center of dramatic moments, but when a situation demands precision and lethality, he is consistently the one who delivers it.

---

## Personality
**Traits:**
- Operates independently and quietly; defaults to reconnaissance over confrontation, scouting ahead with minimal fuss and retreating cleanly when discovered.
- Explosive in first strikes — his opening volleys in ambushes are often the most damage-decisive moment of an encounter, and he positions himself carefully to maximize that.
- Self-sufficient and low-maintenance; he gathers his own information, makes his own purchases (a gold chain, quiet and personal), and rarely needs the party's attention or support.

**Ideals:**
- Precision over power: Kaneko's approach to problems — whether scouting a goliath camp from a tree or sniping a witch to relieve overwhelmed allies — reflects a preference for doing the right thing in the right moment rather than throwing force at everything.
- Vigilance as a duty: as a Gloom Stalker, Kaneko's instinct is to watch the darkness before it watches him, and he acts on threat awareness faster than most.

**Bonds:**
- Vutha — the two have been consistently paired as the advance scouting team, and their partnership dispatched a hag so swiftly in the Cradle of Smoke that the rest of the party was stunned.
- The party at large, expressed through protective precision: when Kormak and Ophie were overwhelmed, Kaneko sniped the threat off them without being asked.

**Flaws:**
- His independence can tip into isolation — slipping away from the group mid-mission to gather information on his own works until it doesn't, and it puts him outside the party's awareness when something goes wrong.
- He has been knocked unconscious (by a firebolt in the temple escape) and thrown overboard (by the Aboleth), suggesting that his survival depends heavily on positioning — if that fails, he has few fallback options.
