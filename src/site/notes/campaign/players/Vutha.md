# [[campaign/players/Vutha\|Vutha]]
A lizardfolk ranger from Llorgh who watched the goliath brothers drive his people from their home, Vutha joined the party with a purpose that is quiet, focused, and personal: he wants the goliaths dead. He does not talk about it dramatically — he acts on it, offering the party secret tunnel routes, leading them through dangerous underground passages, and scouting ahead wherever he can be most useful. Vutha is efficient and lethal, capable of dispatching enemies so quickly that the rest of the party is left stunned, and his combination of Pass Without Trace, ranged precision, and forward scouting with Kaneko makes him an invaluable contributor in nearly every encounter. He has also shown a practical willingness to attempt negotiation where others would not — he spoke directly to the rot dragon in Draconic trying to avoid the fight — though when diplomacy fails he adapts without hesitation. His claim of 93 obsidian hexagons from the rat dragon's hoard, currency of his people in Oman, hints that Vutha is still building toward something beyond revenge.

---

## Personality
**Traits:**
- Efficient and decisive in combat — dispatches enemies with striking speed and precision, frequently working in tandem with Kaneko as a mobile scouting and ranged-damage pair.
- Tactically generous with the party — uses Pass Without Trace on the group, finds hidden levers and passages, and volunteers to scout dangerous areas before the rest advance.
- Pragmatic and calm under pressure; attempts to negotiate or de-escalate when possible (speaking Draconic to the rot dragon), but does not hesitate when violence becomes necessary.

**Ideals:**
- Revenge against the goliaths who drove his people from Llorgh is his primary motivation — not out of rage, but as a patient, purposeful goal he works toward steadily.
- Loyalty to his people and their way of life; he is rebuilding something lost, not just burning down what took it.

**Bonds:**
- His people, once of Llorgh and now displaced to Oman, are the reason he is on this path at all.
- Has formed a working trust with Kaneko, the two of them regularly paired for scouting and flanking throughout the dungeon beneath Llorgh.

**Flaws:**
- The party — particularly Obold — does not fully trust him, given a general distrust of lizardfolk, which creates an undercurrent of tension despite Vutha's demonstrated reliability.
- Was knocked to death saving throws by the rot dragon's breath weapon, suggesting he will push into dangerous positions without fully accounting for his own vulnerability.
